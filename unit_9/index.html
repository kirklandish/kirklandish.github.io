about

Monograph is a project of James Kirkland, whose earliest memory of a sound is listening to the decay of one struck piano note and noticing the naunces of the harmonic spectrum as the note decayed. They utilize analog and digital hardware, software, field recordings, and other found sounds and see technology as a way to delve deeper into the properites of sound.
